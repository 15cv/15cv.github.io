Who is 15cV?

Fifteen Cents Ventures (15cV) is a tech-enabled impact fund reimagining how capital flows into the Creative, Care, and Cooperative Economies. We exist for founders who have been historically excluded from wealth-building — BIPOC, LGBTQIA+, neurodivergent, and disabled entrepreneurs — and for community investors who want to participate in the upside of their success without reproducing extractive capital models.

What we're doing right now

Current work across 15cV and EJL includes:

  • Designing evaluation and data systems for community wealth building initiatives
  • Supporting movement resourcing infrastructure and collaborative learning processes
  • Developing community-centered investment pathways and ecosystem relationships
  • Building research and learning systems that connect impact, accountability, and capital

Current interests and areas of practice:

  • Community investment infrastructure
